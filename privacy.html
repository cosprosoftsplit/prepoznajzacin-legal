Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

PrepoznajZacin ("the App") is developed by SoftSplit d.o.o., Split, Croatia.

Data We Collect

The App processes camera images on-device using Apple's CoreML framework for herb identification. No images are uploaded to any server. All classification happens locally on your device.

Data Stored on Device

  • Identification History - Your scan history is stored locally using SwiftData
  • User Collection - Herbs you save are stored locally
  • Language Preference - Your chosen app language
  • Purchase Status - Whether you have purchased premium (managed by Apple StoreKit)

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob - We display ads to free-tier users. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit - In-app purchases are processed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

We request your permission before any tracking via Apple's ATT framework. You can change this in Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Data Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App removes all associated data.
