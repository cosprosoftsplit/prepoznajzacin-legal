Frequently Asked Questions

How does herb identification work?

Point your camera at a herb and tap the capture button. The app uses on-device machine learning (CoreML) to identify the herb. No internet connection is required for identification.

How accurate is the identification?

Accuracy depends on image quality, lighting, and the herb's condition. The app shows a confidence percentage with each result. For best results, photograph the leaves in good natural light.

What herbs can the app identify?

The app can identify 50 Mediterranean herbs and spices, including basil, rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage, lavender, and many more.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Premium and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Why do I see ads?

Free users see ads to support app development. Upgrade to Premium to remove all ads and unlock all 50 herbs.
